How to Mint

1

Setup

  • Create an account on diba.io and make sure you have installed the BitMask wallet extension
2

Preparation

  • Add BTC to your BitMask non-custodial wallet.
  • Navigate to the Token tab and fund your RGB vault. This prepares your wallet to handle RGB transactions
  • Connect your wallet to diba.io.
3

Mint

  • On the mint day, click 'Summon' and await mempool confirmation.
  • Ensure you have sufficient BTC for a successful mint.
